Innovating with a Healthy Mindset

Yoga is a breath of fresh air to everyone who is committed to finding inner peace and harmon.

  • Sukhasana Or Easy Pose

    It has a relaxing effect on the mind and body which is helpful in reducing stress and anxiety. Improves focus and automatically makes you more attentive. It strengthens the back muscles and improves the overall body posture. Lengthens the spine and back muscles.

  • Naukasana Or Boat Pose

    Naukasana helps in regulating blood flow at sugar level and strengthens the abdominal muscles. It improves the health of all organs in the abdomen, especially the liver, pancreas and kidneys and also strengthens the muscles of the arms, thighs and shoulders.

  • Dhanurasana Or Bow Pose

    Stretches the abdominal muscles and improves the digestion process. Improves the strength in your ankles, thighs, chest, neck and shoulders. Helps tone your abdominal region. Tones your back and improves spinal flexibility. Helps open up your chest region and facilitates better breathing.

  • Vakrasana Or Twisted Pose

    It regulates the secretion of digestive juices, improving digestion and combating constipation. It massages the abdominal organs and helps reduce belly fat. It makes the spine flexible. It strengthens the back and helps with chronic back and shoulder pain..

  • Kakasana Or Crow Pose

    Strengthens arms so they can hold your body weight. Strengthens core to help hold you up. Strengthens glutes and adductors. Strengthens shoulder stabilisers. Prepares you for most other arm balances. It helps make your wrists stronger. Your spine is toned and strengthened.

  • Bhujangasana Or Cobra Stretch

    Strengthens the spine. Stretches chest and lungs, shoulders, and abdomen. Tones the buttocks. Stimulates abdominal organs. Helps relieve stress and fatigue. Opens the heart and lungs. Soothes sciatica. Therapeutic for asthma.
